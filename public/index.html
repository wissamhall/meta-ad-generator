How to Write Meta Ads That Convert

Great Meta ad copy follows proven principles. Here's what separates high-performing ads from the rest.

01

Hook in 3 Words

Users scroll fast. Your first 3 words determine if they stop. Lead with a benefit, question, or bold statement that demands attention.

02

Benefits Over Features

Don't say what your product does â€” say what it does for them. "Save 10 hours/week" beats "automated scheduling tool" every time.

03

One Clear CTA

Every ad needs exactly one action. Multiple CTAs create decision paralysis. Match your CTA to your campaign objective for maximum relevance.

04

Stay Within Limits

Meta truncates long text. Keep primary text under 125 characters, headlines under 40, and descriptions under 30 for full visibility.
